Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you register, along with the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the core features of Spendly: authenticating your account, storing your expenses, and generating summaries and breakdowns of your spending. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or email delivery services) to operate the application. These providers only process data as needed to provide their service to us and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
